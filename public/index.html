Common Questions

What is an AI receptionist, and how is it different from voicemail?

An AI receptionist answers calls live, in real time — it can talk to customers, answer common questions, and book appointments directly into your calendar. Unlike voicemail, callers get an actual response instead of leaving a message and hoping for a callback.

Do you serve businesses outside Calgary?

Yes — we work with small businesses across Alberta, including Calgary and Medicine Hat, and can support clients remotely anywhere. Everything from setup to ongoing support is handled online.

How much does website design cost?

Website projects start at C$597, depending on scope and features needed. Book a free call and we'll give you a clear, upfront quote — no hidden fees.

How long does setup take?

Most AI receptionist setups are live within a few days. Websites typically launch within 1-2 weeks depending on complexity. We'll give you a specific timeline on your free consultation call.
